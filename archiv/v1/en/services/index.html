What I do (and what I don't)

WordPress, WooCommerce, static HTML — depends on what you need. I don't recommend by template, I recommend after understanding how your business works.

01

Build new

Company website, shop or tool — the technology comes after the problem:

Company website

WordPress if your team needs to manage content. Static HTML if speed and zero maintenance matter. Similar setup for Scheja & Partner — law firm, 30 lawyers, three software product pages.

Online shop

WooCommerce when off-the-shelf shops aren't enough. B2B dealer conditions, price groups, sample shipping, custom checkout processes. I rebuilt the shop for Gerwing Steinwerke from scratch in 2023.

Portal or tool

Login areas, dealer portals, order systems, interactive reports. Not every digital problem is a website — sometimes you need something custom. Example: the impact report for SiS.

You know what you need? Get in touch.

02

Make it better

Your website exists but something's off? Redesign, performance, accessibility retrofitting — or all three at once.

Redesign

Not everything that looks dated needs a full relaunch. Sometimes a new layout is enough, sometimes the structure needs work. I look at what's there and tell you honestly what's worth doing.

Performance

Slow websites lose customers and rankings. I find out what's causing it — whether it's the hosting, the images, the theme or a plugin that's dragging everything down.

Accessibility (EAA/BFSG)

Since June 2025, websites must be accessible. Not "should be", must be. Fines up to 100,000 EUR. WCAG 2.1 AA is my standard — and if your existing site doesn't meet it, we'll figure out what needs to happen.

Three years of games QA taught me one thing: finding bugs systematically is a learned profession. That helps here.

Sounds like your problem? A short call sorts the rest.

03
