About

Erik Andersen. In the web since 1999. Multimedia design at Hamburg Technical Art School (2007–2010). QA in the games industry – Goodgame Studios, Deep Silver FISHLABS, InnoGames. Support management at YOOtheme. Frontend development at Tangram ad agency. Self-employed since 2010.

The path led through design, testing, support, development and maintenance – each discipline several years full-time. The result: concept, design, code, QA and ongoing maintenance from a single source.

AI-augmented

Since 2025, I work extensively with AI tools. Code generation, testing, research. What AI doesn't handle: strategy, concept, design decisions, quality assurance.

Language quality

My wife Annemarie is a freelance editor and translator (annemarie-andersen.de). Website copy that nobody understands usually hasn't been edited. In my projects, editing is built in.
