B2B / Industry · WooCommerce

Gerwing Steinwerke

gerwing.de →

Gerwing manufactures paving stones – in a league where hardware stores buy from them. When I joined in 2023, the existing shop needed a fundamental redesign: better user guidance, a working sample ordering system, clear paths for dealers and end customers.

I rebuilt the entire shop from scratch. WooCommerce as the base, but heavily customized: a stone sample ordering system with email tracking, product filters by color, format, and application area, price groups for different customer types.

The result: A shop that serves both audiences – end customers who want inspiration, and dealers who need to order quickly. Since then, I maintain the shop alone. One contact, short paths, direct implementation. → More about online shops

Corporate Group · 11 Websites · WordPress

Runden Group

runden-group.eu →

Runden Group is a family business with subsidiaries in recycling, logistics, IT, building planning, and agriculture. Each company needed its own website – uniform in appearance, but individual in content.

I built the entire web presence: 11 websites on WordPress with YOOtheme Pro, each bilingual (DE/EN), plus a career portal and a podcast section. The designs came as Figma mockups – everything else I derived and implemented.

For two years, I was solely responsible for the entire web presence of this corporate group. Updates, content maintenance, new features, technical issues – all came across my desk. This is the project I mention when someone says: "One person can't possibly maintain a corporate group." → More about project management & frontend

Legal / Data Privacy · WordPress · multilingual

Scheja & Partner

scheja-partners.de →

A data privacy law firm with 30 attorneys in Bonn – specialized in compliance, NIS2, and GDPR. The requirement: a website that not only presents the firm but also sells their software products.

I built the law firm website and three product pages for their privacy software. Everything bilingual, with structured data for legal services, so the firm appears correctly in AI search systems as a data privacy specialist.

The project shows what happens when you don't understand web development as "building a pretty site" but as a tool for business development. The website works for the firm – not just as a business card. → More about web design & consulting
