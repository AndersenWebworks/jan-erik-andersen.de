About remote work

"Can you come by sometime?" – The most common question. The honest answer:

My preferred mode is remote – but if you insist, I'll meet you in person too. Since 2010, I've been working for clients in Hamburg, Bonn, Osnabrück, Vechta, Landshut – without ever being on-site. Not a single one said the result suffered because of it.

Video calls have advantages that in-person meetings don't: screen sharing, we're both at computers, more flexible scheduling. At an on-site meeting, you're sitting with pen and paper, I'm on a small laptop screen – and at the end everything still needs to be summarized digitally anyway.

Think about it the other way: Most agencies work remotely internally now. Their developers work from home. You still pay for the office, the reception, and the meeting room where you drink your coffee. Enjoy your coffee at home – and invest the savings in the website. Why one person is more efficient →
