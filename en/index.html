The Methodology

I don't build websites.
I build machine-readable data structures into your existing website.

This means:

  • Semantic HTML: Every term gets a unique meaning.
  • Structured Data: JSON-LD, Schema.org, Microdata – what AI agents understand.
  • Consistent terminology: No synonyms, no variations.
  • Clear hierarchies: What belongs where. What's important.

For humans, your website looks the same as before.
For AI agents, it's a precise data schema.
