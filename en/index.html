Who's behind this

I've worked at agencies. I know what they look like from the inside. That's why I do it differently.

My background is Multimedia Design – I think in layouts and user guidance, not algorithms. After graduation, I deliberately chose the games industry: Quality Assurance at Goodgame Studios, FISHLABS, and Daedalic. Three years of professionally finding bugs before users see them. That shapes you.

In 2017, I worked at YOOtheme – the maker of the page builder I still use today. I helped write their documentation and have worked with the system daily for 9 years.

After that, frontend development at an advertising agency – but I already had agency experience before that through internships and client projects during my studies. That's where I saw what clients pay for: office space, account managers, coordination meetings. Development itself was a fraction of the invoice. Since 2023, I've been exclusively freelance – and that insight is my business model.

My wife Annemarie is a proofreader. Every text that goes out under my name is professionally edited. That's not a bonus – it's part of the package.
