What I do

Five things, and those right. I don't do everything – but what I do, I do well.

01

Web Design

Company websites that work – not just look good. WordPress for clients who want to manage content themselves. Static HTML for maximum speed and minimal maintenance. Responsive, GDPR compliant. I think from the goal: What should the site achieve for you? Then we build backwards.

Example

Scheja & Partner – Data privacy law firm with 30 attorneys, multilingual, with product pages for their privacy software.

WordPress Static HTML Responsive WCAG 2.1 AA
02

Online Shops

WooCommerce shops designed from the start with clear processes. Product management, variants, filters, checkout – I build the system so you can operate it without constantly calling me. B2B features like dealer conditions and price groups are no magic when you know what you're doing.

Example

Gerwing Steinwerke – WooCommerce shop for one of Germany's largest paving stone manufacturers, completely rebuilt in 2023 with sample ordering system and price groups.

WooCommerce B2B / B2C Germanized E-Invoicing
03

Frontend Development

Clean solutions, documented and maintainable. If I get hit by a bus, any other developer can continue with it. I implement designs – whether from Figma, Sketch, or a napkin sketch. Performance is not a bonus but a baseline requirement. Lighthouse scores of 90+ are standard.

Example

Runden Group – complete web presence of a corporate group (11 websites), bilingual custom plugins, career portal, client portal.

HTML / CSS / JS YOOtheme Pro Performance Figma → Code
04

Project Management & Consulting

You have a project but no plan? Or a plan but nobody to implement and manage it? I take on both. Concept development, requirements analysis, implementation management – and if needed, also communication with your other service providers. No Jira, no Slack chaos, no overhead.

Example

Runden Group – two years sole responsibility for 11 websites of a corporate group including coordination with external design team.

Concepts Management Tech Consulting Vendor Coordination
05

Website Analysis

You have a website but it's not delivering? I look at what's causing that. Performance, SEO, accessibility, security, user guidance – all measurable, all concrete. No vague recommendations, but a report with clear priorities and effort estimates.

Three years in games QA taught me: Finding errors systematically is a learned profession.
